Course We Offer

This College provides quality education offering the best groups like MPC, BiPC, and CEC for student's academic growth.

M.P.C

At Holy Cross Junior College, the MPC (Maths, Physics, Chemistry) group is designed for students aiming at careers in engineering, technology, and related fields. The program emphasizes strong analytical and problem-solving skills through rigorous theoretical and practical learning, preparing students for competitive exams and higher education opportunities.

Bi.P.C

At Holy Cross Junior College, the BiPC (Biology, Physics, Chemistry) group focuses on building a solid foundation in life sciences. It prepares students for careers in medicine, pharmacy, agriculture, and allied fields while enhancing scientific knowledge through practical learning and academic excellence.

CEC

At Holy Cross Junior College, the CEC (Civics, Economics, Commerce) group equips students with knowledge in social sciences, business, and governance. It helps them develop analytical, managerial, and communication skills, preparing for careers in commerce, law, administration, and higher education.
